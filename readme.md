
# Akan Name Generator

A simple tool to generate traditional Akan names based on the day of the week you were born.

## About

The Akan people of Ghana and Ivory Coast have a naming tradition where children are named after the day of the week they were born. This generator helps you discover your Akan name.

## Features

- Generate Akan names by day of birth
- Learn about Akan naming traditions
- Simple and intuitive interface

## How to Use

1. Select the day of the week you were born
2. Choose your gender (Male/Female)
3. Click "Generate Name" to see your Akan name

## Installation

No installation required. Open in your web browser.
